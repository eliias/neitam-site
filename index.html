About

neti.am aims to provider a fully-featured open-source API stack for your next project. Using progressive and easy to use technology (Node.js, MongoDB), we try to shorten down your development time and reduce your costs.

We do our best to give you a solid and straight-forward API next to a modular system, so that this will fit easily into most of your development scenarios.
